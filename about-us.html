WHO WE ARE

NETCO, a full-service EPCI (Engineering, procurement, construction, & installation), Operations & maintenance company that provides innovative, reliable, and cost-effective energy solutions to clients across various industries. We have a proven track record of delivering high-quality projects that meet our clients requirements while staying within the budget and timeline. Our experiences of over 30 years in the field has given us the knowledge and expertise to tackle complex projects of all sizes.
