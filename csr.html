Education Programme

NETCO GOES TO SCHOOLS

Through its "NETCO Goes to Schools" programme, NETCO's aim is to inspire and enable the future generation of innovators and problem solvers in addition to demystifying the study of engineering and STEM (science, technology, engineering, and mathematics) courses. Our aim is to instil a passion for learning and discovery among secondary school students while showcasing in them the exciting opportunities within the fields of Engineering and STEM.

NETCO organises engaging and interactive sessions, including presentations, seminars, and hands-on activities, tailored to the students' age and educational levels. Our team of experienced engineers, scientists, and professionals provide insights into various engineering disciplines, illustrating real-world applications and career options.

Donations

DONATIONS TO INSTITUTIONS AND COMMUNITIES

Alongside our structured programs, NETCO actively engages in donations to institutions and communities as part of our broader commitment to societal welfare. Notably, we have sponsored the Hebron Motor Sports Team, showcasing our dedication to supporting local initiatives and sporting endeavors. Also, during the challenging times of the COVID-19 lockdown, NETCO rose to the occasion by giving support to various segments of society, understanding the hardships faced by individuals, communities, and frontline workers.

NETCO distributed relief packages comprising essential food items, hygiene supplies, and personal protective equipment (PPE) to vulnerable communities severely affected by the lockdown measures. These distributions were carried out in collaboration with local authorities and community leaders to ensure efficient and fair distribution. At NETCO, our CSR activities are deeply ingrained in our corporate ethos, reflecting our steadfast dedication to social responsibility and sustainable community development.
